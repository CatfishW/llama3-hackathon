Help & Guide

Getting Started

Select a project, type your message, and press Enter to send. The AI assistant will respond in real-time.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Enter: Send message
  • Shift+Enter: New line
  • Ctrl+K: New chat
  • Ctrl+L: Clear chat

  • Top-P: Controls diversity of word choices
  • Max Tokens: Maximum length of response
